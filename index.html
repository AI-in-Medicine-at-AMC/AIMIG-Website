Who We Are

Medicine meets machine intelligence.

AIMIG is Albany Medical College's student-led organization dedicated to exploring the intersection of artificial intelligence and clinical medicine. We believe every future physician should understand the tools reshaping healthcare.


Whether you want to understand AI, apply it clinically, or build with it — this is your starting point.

01
Clinical Understanding
Learn how AI is being used in diagnostics, imaging, pathology, and patient care right now.
02
Critical Thinking
Understand the limitations, biases, and ethical challenges of AI in medicine — not just the hype.
03
Hands-On Building
For those who want to go deeper — workshops, coding sessions, and research opportunities.
